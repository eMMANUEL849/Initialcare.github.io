Our Services

Support shaped around the person, not the diagnosis.

Every care plan starts with a conversation, not a form. Below is what we currently offer as we launch — your plan may combine several of these.

Personal care

Washing, dressing, continence support, and help with mobility — delivered with dignity and always the same familiar face.

Medication support

Prompts, administration, and clear records, so families and GPs always know what's been taken and when.

Companionship visits

Conversation, a shared cup of tea, a walk outside — for people whose main need is simply not being alone.

Meal preparation

Fresh, simple meals cooked in your kitchen, with attention to dietary needs and personal taste.

Live-in care

A carer based in the home around the clock, for people who need more continuous support but want to stay in familiar surroundings.

Respite care

Short-term cover so family carers can rest, travel, or simply take a break, with no disruption to routine.

How it starts

From enquiry to first visit

  • A free home assessment — we visit, listen, and understand what a good day actually looks like for you.
  • A written care plan — agreed with you and, where relevant, your family and GP.
  • A matched carer — introduced before their first solo visit, not sent in cold.
  • Regular reviews — care needs change, and so does the plan.

Not sure which service fits?

Tell us a little about the situation and we'll suggest a starting point — no pressure, no obligation.
