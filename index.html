FAQ

Questions people ask before choosing home care.

How quickly can I arrange my first visit?

We aim to arrange an introductory visit within 3–5 working days, depending on your location and care needs.

Will I always see the same carer?

Yes, we prioritise consistent carers and only change the support worker when absolutely necessary.

What happens if I need to cancel a visit?

We ask for as much notice as possible and will help rearrange the visit without compromising continuity of care.

A typical visit day

Support that fits around real life.

Care can be booked as a single daily visit or several across the day — mornings, afternoons and evenings each cover something different.

Morning

Getting started

Help getting up, washing and dressing, breakfast, and medication prompts — starting the day well.

Afternoon

Staying independent

Light meal preparation, a walk or errands, companionship, and help with household tasks.

Evening

Winding down

Support getting ready for bed, a final medication check, and making sure the home is safe overnight.
