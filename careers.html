Careers

Care work, done properly — and valued properly.

We're building our founding team of carers. If you're patient, reliable, and take pride in the small things, we'd like to hear from you — experience helpful but not always essential.

Fair, realistic rotas

Visit times that reflect the work involved, and travel time between calls that's actually paid.

Full training provided

Induction covers moving and handling, medication, safeguarding, and first aid — paid, not unpaid.

A team, not a gig

Regular contact with a care coordinator, consistent clients, and a say in how your rounds are built.

What we look for

You don't need a care background to apply.

What matters most: reliability, kindness under pressure, and a willingness to be trained properly. A full enhanced DBS check and right-to-work in the UK are required for every role.
