Compliance & Safety

Being trustworthy is the whole job. Here's how we prove it.

Current CQC status: registration in progress.

Any organisation providing regulated personal care in England must register with the Care Quality Commission (CQC) before delivering that care. Initial Care is completing this registration process now. We will update this page, and confirm to every client directly, once registration is granted.

Safeguarding

Every carer completes safeguarding training before their first visit and knows how to recognise and report concerns. We follow local authority safeguarding procedures and take every concern seriously, however small.

DBS checks

All staff undergo an enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check, renewed periodically, before working unsupervised with clients.

Training standards

Induction covers moving and handling, medication administration, first aid, infection control, and person-centred care — aligned with the Care Certificate framework used across the UK care sector.

Data protection

Client records are held securely and in line with UK GDPR. Information is only shared with people directly involved in a client's care, or where we're legally required to.

Questions about our compliance?

Ask us directly — we'd rather you knew than wondered.
