Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Last updated: 3 July 2026. This page explains what we collect when you get in touch, and what we do with it.

What we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our Contact page, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email address, who you're enquiring for, and any message you write.

We don't use tracking cookies or third-party analytics on this site. The only cookie we set is a short-lived, secure login session for our own admin staff when they sign in to view enquiries — it isn't used to track visitors.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry — contacting you back by phone or email to discuss care needs.
  • To arrange care — building a care plan if you choose to proceed with us.
  • Nothing else. We don't sell your details, and we don't use them for marketing you haven't asked for.
Storage & access

Where your enquiry is kept.

Enquiries are stored in a secure database and are only visible to authorised Initial Care staff, who log in with a username and password to view them. We keep enquiry records for as long as reasonably needed to respond to you and maintain accurate business records, and delete them when they're no longer needed.

Your rights

You're in control of your data.

Under UK data protection law, you can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. To do any of these, email us at Initialcarehomes@outlook.com and we'll respond as soon as we can.

Questions about your data?

Get in touch and we'll talk you through it — no jargon.
